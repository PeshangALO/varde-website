About us

Varde AS is an innovative tech company specializing in developing explainable AI solutions to empower people with diabetes. Our team combines deep expertise in AI and personal experience with diabetes to create cutting-edge technology.

1

Our Goal

We aim to reduce illness and improve the quality of life for all people with diabetes by providing intelligent, user-friendly tools that enable better control and management of their condition.

2

What is Varde?

We've developed an AI algorithm that predicts glucose levels and explains its insulin adjustment recommendations. By integrating 42 factors, our solution provides personalized insights, empowering users to manage diabetes more confidently through an intuitive app.
